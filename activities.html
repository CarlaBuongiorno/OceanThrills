Surfing

Waves, sun, and some of the best surfing around, discover great spots in Mauritius for waves and memories that will stay with you forever.

Snorkeling

Explore the various bio diversities around the island, make a unique experience for all experiences with amazing coral and rare sights to behold.

Swim With Dolphins

With great experiences to be had, there are numerous operators who can accommodate your swimming with the dolphins. The waters are clear and the dolphins friendly. This is a must do activity for the whole family.

Kite Surfing

The diverse kite surfing and foiling opportunities in the many great spots around the island cannot be missed. You just can't beat the reliable conditions at most spots.

Scuba Diving

Corals, wrecks, and outer island experiences aplenty. The vast choice of biospheres, dive types, and excellent conditions makes Mauritius a scuba diving smorgasbord of prize choices.
