Surfing

Keen to ride the waves, bask in the sun, and hang ten? There are plenty of great spots on the coast for you to hit the waves and explore. If you’re wondering where you can catch the perfect waves, don’t fret! Beyond the reef lies a surfer’s dream location. Below you will find twelve of the best spots to surf in Mauritius. 

Snorkeling

Explore the magical corals and marine life of Mauritius, an experience you will never forget! The reefs in Mauritius are spectacular. Snorkelling allows you to get closer to the marvellous realms of underwater, making it one of the finest sea activities.

Swim With Dolphins

Seeing animals in their natural habitat is always better than seeing them in captivity. Period. No matter how ‘nice’ the aquariums you go to are, it’s always, always better to be able to witnesses animals “up-close” in a place where they are wild and free - in this case, the ocean. If you embark on an excursion to go swimming with dolphins in Mauritius, you will (if you are lucky enough to come across them) be able to see these gorgeous creatures in all of their glory. Generally, these animals travel in pods of 20 or more and tend to be seen around Tamarin Bay (in the west of Mauritius) in the early hours of the morning as they make their way to deeper waters around Le Morne peninsula to feed. The dolphin species in Mauritius that are most commonly seen are the Bottlenose and Spinner dolphins which amaze and surprise onlookers with their playful personalities as they gracefully surf the waves.

Kite Surfing

Mauritius is one of the best destinations in the world for kitesurfing. There are fantastic surf spots all around the island but it’s the south that is renowned for strong and consistent tradewinds, especially between April and November. One Eye at Pointe du Morne or Cap Dal in Tamarin are the island’s most famous kitesurfing areas, so you can learn in the lagoon or refine your pro moves on the perfect barrel wave. We’ll see you soon in the surf…

Scuba Diving

In Mauritius, the scuba diving scene is absolutely fascinating. Enjoy the colorful marine life and the peaceful silence of the sea as you dive into it. And this sport is not limited to the diving experts only. Non-swimmers can scuba dive as well, but if you know how to swim, scuba diving in Mauritius can be an exhilarating experience.
